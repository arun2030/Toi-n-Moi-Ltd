public International Orders

We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your location and the weight of your order.

Note: Duties and taxes are not included in the checkout total. You may be responsible for paying these fees upon delivery depending on your country's regulations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a refund? expand_more
Once we receive your return, please allow 3-5 business days for our team to inspect and process the item. Refunds typically appear on your original payment method within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank.
Can I exchange an item for a different size? expand_more
Yes! The fastest way to ensure you get what you want is to return the item you have, and once the return is accepted, make a separate purchase for the new item. Alternatively, use our returns portal to select "Exchange" for a direct swap if available.
What if I received a damaged item? expand_more
We're so sorry about that! Please contact our support team immediately with your order number and a photo of the damage. We'll send out a replacement right away at no extra cost to you.
