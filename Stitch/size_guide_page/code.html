How to Measure

For the most accurate fit, take your measurements while wearing minimal clothing. Keep the tape measure level and snug, but not tight.

  • 1

    Bust

    Measure around the fullest part of your chest.

  • 2

    Waist

    Measure at the narrowest part of your waistline.

  • 3

    Hips

    Measure at the fullest part of your hips while standing with feet together.
