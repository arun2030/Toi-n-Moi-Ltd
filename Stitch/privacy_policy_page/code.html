Introduction

Welcome to Toi & Moi. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website (regardless of where you visit it from) and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together follows:

badge

Identity Data

Includes first name, maiden name, last name, username or similar identifier, marital status, title, date of birth and gender.

call

Contact Data

Includes billing address, delivery address, email address and telephone numbers.

payments

Financial Data

Includes bank account and payment card details. Note: We do not store full credit card numbers.

devices

Technical Data

Includes internet protocol (IP) address, your login data, browser type and version, time zone setting and location.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to distinguish you from other users of our website. This helps us to provide you with a good experience when you browse our website and also allows us to improve our site. You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when websites set or access cookies.
