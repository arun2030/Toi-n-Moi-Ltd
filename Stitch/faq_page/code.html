How long does standard shipping take? expand_more

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. For international orders, please allow 7-14 business days depending on customs processing in your country.

Do you ship internationally? expand_more

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location and are calculated at checkout. Please note that duties and taxes are not included in the shipping price.

How can I track my order? expand_more

Once your order ships, you’ll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also view your order status by logging into your account and visiting the Orders page.

What happens if my package is lost? expand_more

If your package hasn't arrived within the expected timeframe, please verify the tracking information. If it appears lost, contact our support team within 14 days of the estimated delivery date, and we will assist you in filing a claim.
